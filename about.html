Our Story

Founded in 2001 by Dr. Augustine Akalonu, Community Pediatrics was built on a simple promise: to treat every child in the Bronx like our own family. What started as a small one-room office has grown into a pillar of the community, serving thousands of families with compassionate, culturally competent care.
